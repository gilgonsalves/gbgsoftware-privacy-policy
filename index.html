GBGSoftware Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

GBGSoftware respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile applications collect, use, and protect information.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed to collect as little personal information as possible. Most GBGSoftware apps do not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

Some anonymous information may be collected automatically through third-party services used within our apps.

Analytics

Some GBGSoftware apps use Google Firebase Analytics to help us understand how users interact with our apps. This information is collected anonymously and may include device type, operating system version, general app usage, app performance information, and crash reports.

Advertising

Some GBGSoftware apps display advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain anonymous information to provide and improve advertising services.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Some GBGSoftware apps do not display advertisements.

Local Data Storage

Many of our apps store game progress, statistics, settings, or preferences locally on your device. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to GBGSoftware.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use services provided by Google Firebase, Google AdMob, and Apple App Store. These services have their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are intended for a general audience and do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated Effective Date.
